I grew up between two worlds. Tamil cinema songs filled the living room, weekends were spent at classical Indian dance and music lessons, and the sounds of Timbaland, Jay-Kay, Missy Elliott, and The Neptunes spilling out of my brother’s bedroom. My music is an eclectic, sometimes fractured mix of these sounds.
