Dilush Selva is a UK-based Tamil music producer, composer, multidisciplinary creative working across sound and moving image. His work blends downtempo electronic, ambient, and alt-R&B influences with intricate South Asian melodies, alongside abstract collage and storytelling rooted in diaspora & migrant experiences.

His practice spans cinematic scores and independent releases, with releases on Soulection, Sony Music South, and Saregama, and music featured on BBC Radio 1 and BBC Asian Network.
